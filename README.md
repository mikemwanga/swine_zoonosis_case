### Abstract
We report pig-to-human transmission of Eurasian-avian swine H1N1 influenza A virus (IAV) from a herd to their farmer, both presenting with influenza-like illness symptoms. Whole genome analysis revealed >99.9% sequence identity between hosts. This underscores the zoonotic potential of enzootic swine IAV and highlights the relevance of active epidemiological and genomic surveillance.
